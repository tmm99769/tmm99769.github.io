Thomas Muller

Thomas Muller is an incredible athlete, scholar, and chef. Hold your horses, I'm not talking about the world renowned soccer player, Thomas Müller. This is the other Muller. The one you don't see in the news. This Thomas Muller was born on April 20th, 1999 in Atlanta, Georgia. He was raised in the suburbs of Atlanta and attended North Atlanta High School from 2013 - 2017. After that, he was accepted into the University of Georgia, and the rest is history.

The other Thomas Müller is still a confirmed legend. He is the unanimous golden boot winner of the 2010 World Cup with 5 goals and 3 assists. He is one of the youngest players of all time to accomplish such a feat. Thomas Müller is now 29 years old but is still in peak condition. He has stayed loyal to his club Bayern Munich for his entire career and has experienced many ups, and even more downs.

My favorite games played by Thomas Muller are...

  2. VS. Argentina 2010
  3. VS. Wolfsburg 2015
